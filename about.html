The Problem & Solution

Bridging the Gap from Farm to Market

Smallholder farmers in rural Sierra Leone often lack direct access to markets and real-time pricing information. This leads to post-harvest losses and exploitation by middlemen.

Salone FarmHub is a Digital Public Good that connects farmers directly with wholesale buyers and local vendors. Our platform guarantees transparent pricing, reducing waste and increasing farmer incomes.

  • Direct connections to buyers
  • Reduced post-harvest loss
  • Increased profit margins
Our Mission

Empowering Every Farmer

Our mission is to eliminate information asymmetry in Sierra Leone's agricultural sector by providing accessible, real-time market data to every smallholder farmer.

Our Vision

A Sustainable Future

We envision a thriving, self-sustaining agricultural economy where farmers are fairly compensated, and post-harvest waste is completely eliminated through efficient market connections.
