## Overview
**Salone FarmHub** is a conceptual Digital Public Good (DPG) designed to address the challenges faced by smallholder farmers in rural Sierra Leone. The platform aims to bridge the gap from farm to market by connecting farmers directly with wholesale buyers and local vendors. 

By providing access to real-time pricing information and direct market connections, Salone FarmHub reduces post-harvest losses and empowers farmers to negotiate fair prices, ultimately increasing their incomes and fostering a sustainable agricultural economy.

## Problem & Solution
Smallholder farmers in Sierra Leone often lack direct access to markets and real-time pricing information. This leads to post-harvest losses and exploitation by middlemen. 

**Salone FarmHub** solves this by:
- Creating direct connections between farmers and buyers.
- Reducing post-harvest loss through faster farm-to-market pipelines.
- Increasing profit margins for rural farmers by ensuring transparent pricing.

## Key Features
- **Real-Time Pricing:** Farmers can access up-to-date market prices via USSD or the mobile app.
- **Direct Trade:** Instant connections with verified wholesale buyers, cutting out exploitative middlemen.
- **Logistics Support:** Tools to coordinate transport and delivery schedules to ensure fresh produce reaches the market safely.

## SDG Alignment
Our Digital Public Good is deeply committed to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs):
- **SDG 1: No Poverty** - Empowering farmers to access wider markets and transparent pricing to significantly increase household income.
- **SDG 2: Zero Hunger** - Reducing post-harvest loss to ensure more food is available for the population.
- **SDG 8: Decent Work & Economic Growth** - Fostering a more robust, fair, and modern agricultural economy that supports decent livelihoods for rural communities.
